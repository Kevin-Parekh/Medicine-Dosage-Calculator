Standard Strength Guidance

Commonly used concentrations to help you identify the correct value for your medication.

Medicine Target Group Standard Dosage Strength Clinical Context
Paracetamol Infants / Toddlers 120mg / 5ml (24mg/ml) Small volume concentration for safer newborn/infant measuring.
Paracetamol Children (6+ yrs) 250mg / 5ml (50mg/ml) "Six Plus" suspension; less liquid required for older children.
Ibuprofen Pediatric General 100mg / 5ml (20mg/ml) The most frequent over-the-counter (OTC) preparation.
Amoxicillin Antibiotic Choice 250mg / 5ml (50mg/ml) Often referred to as the "Double Strength" (DS) formulation.
General Tablets Pills / Caplets Varies (e.g. 500mg) Check the specific mg value per individual single tablet.

Always verify your specific bottle's concentration. Look for "X mg per 5ml" or "X mg/ml" on the medicine label.
